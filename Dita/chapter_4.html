Chapter 4

After all of the pigs built their homes, they were very happy. The straw home, wooden home, and brick home all seemed very safe and sturdy. Then, one day, a big bad wolf came walking down the road. The wolf saw the straw house first.

The wolf knocked on the door and said, "Let me in, little pig, let me in!"

"Not by the hair on my chinny, chin, chin!" replied the little pig.

"Then I will huff and puff and BLOW your house down!" growled the wolf.

The wolf huffed and puffed and blew the house down. As the pig tried to run away, he tripped on all the fallen straw. The wolf caught the pig and ate him. No one would have ever known a pig used to live there.
