Chapter 1

Once upon a time, there were three little pigs that still lived at home with their parents. One day, the mom pig told the three little pigs they needed to find their own homes. So, the three little pigs each set out on a journey to find a home of their own.

The pigs whined and complained as they helped one another search for a way to build a new home.

As the three little pigs walked to find supplies to build their houses, the first little pig saw a farmer with some straw.

The pig decided straw would work great for the house he planned to build. The little pig offered to buy the farmer a drink in exchange for some straw. The farmer happily excepted the offer, they drank all night. The next day the pig began to build his straw house.
