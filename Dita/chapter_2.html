Chapter 2

After the first pig stopped to build a house out of straw, the two other little pigs continued walking.

The pigs whined and complained as they helped one another search for a way to build a new home.

The second pig finally saw a woodcutter with a lot of wood.

The second pig thought wood would be great to build his home and he knew he had to have it. So, to ensure the woodcutter did not refuse, the pigs worked together steal the wood.

The pigs snuck up behind the woodcutter and hit him in the back of the head, knocking him out. They then stole the wood and went to build the second little piggy's house.
